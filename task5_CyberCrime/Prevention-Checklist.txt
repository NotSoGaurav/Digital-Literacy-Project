Title: Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

General Safety Tips:
- Always verify the sender’s email address before trusting any message.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links received via email or SMS.
- Check the URL of websites carefully before entering login details.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Keep your devices and software updated regularly.
- Do not download files or attachments from unknown sources.
- Be cautious of messages that create urgency or fear.

UPI and Financial Safety:
- Always double-check the UPI ID before sending money.
- Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking credentials with anyone.
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.
- Use official apps for financial transactions.

Social Media Safety:
- Do not share personal information publicly on social media.
- Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your posts.
- Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people.

Reporting Cybercrime:
- Report cybercrime incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Contact helpline number: 1930 (available 24x7)

Conclusion:
Following these simple precautions can help individuals protect themselves from cyber threats and ensure safe use of digital platforms.
